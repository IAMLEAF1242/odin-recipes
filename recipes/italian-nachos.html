Italian Nachos

Description:

These italian nachos are simple to make and not to mention delicious. The dish is a sort of mashup between chicken alfredo with noodles and nachos. it features a crunchy satisfying texture and a variety of flavors when consumed with some good toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Potato chips
  • alfredo sauce
  • chicken
  • olives
  • pepperoncini

Steps:

  1. cook chicken in a pan
  2. shred the chicken
  3. stir in alfredo sauce
  4. serve sauce with potato chips
  5. add toppings, Enjoy
