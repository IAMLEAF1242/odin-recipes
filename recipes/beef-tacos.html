Beef Tacos

Description:

It only takes 6 ingredients and 15 minutes to make this ground beef taco recipe. Mexican Ground beef tacos are an easy weeknight meal that the entire family loves!

Ingredients:

  • ground beef
  • taco seasoning
  • onion
  • minced garlic
  • lime juice
  • tortillas

Steps:

  1. Cook the ground beef with onion and garlic
  2. add the taco seasoning and stir
  3. add lime juice and keep cooking until brown
  4. serve sauce with potato chips
  5. put mixture onto a torilla, Enjoy!
